chicken gyros

Description

This loaf pan chicken gyros comes out moist and tender and picks up lots of flavor from the yogurt marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup plain whole milk Greek yogurt/li>
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a 9x5-inch loaf pan with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk together yogurt, lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, paprika, dill, and pepper in a large bowl. Add chicken and toss to coat.
  3. Layer chicken thighs in the prepared loaf pan.
